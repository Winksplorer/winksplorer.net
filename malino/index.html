malino is an OS toolkit.

You can make your own operating system with malino. Get everything in your computer, exactly how you want it.

Based on Linux.

Because we have a good kernel (unlike Cosmos), there is actually potential that malino OSes could be a daily driver.

Easy and quick too.

While currently we only support the V programming language, we have plans to support tons of languages soon.
